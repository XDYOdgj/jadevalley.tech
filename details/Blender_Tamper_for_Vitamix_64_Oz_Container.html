GahsElec Blender Tamper

Perfectly designed for use with Vitamix Classic Standard 64 oz. containers, this blender tamper is a reliable replacement part that ensures optimal performance. It allows food to be forced into the processing blades without direct contact, reducing the risk of contamination and prolonging the life of your blender.

Easy to clean and built with durability in mind, this tamper is an ideal accessory for home cooks and food enthusiasts. Each tamper stick is individually wrapped in a polybag, providing a hygienic and secure packaging solution that guarantees quality and safety.

Compatible with Vitamix models 760, 5000, and 5200, this component is engineered to fit standard 64 oz. containers precisely. It is not suitable for professional series models like 300, 750, or 7500. Ideal for those seeking cost-effective, high-performance replacement parts without compromising on quality.
