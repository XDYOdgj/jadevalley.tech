Oliboni K90 Pitcher Replacement

Upgrade your blending experience with this high-quality 80oz pitcher replacement, designed to work seamlessly with the Oliboni B-90A, WantJoin K90, and CRANDDI K90 blenders. This food-grade pitcher combines durability and functionality to make your kitchen tasks easier and more enjoyable.

Key Features
  • Perfectly fits Oliboni B-90A, WantJoin K90, and CRANDDI K90 blenders
  • Large 2200ml capacity for making smoothies, purees, and shakes
  • Constructed with durable polycarbonate (PC) for long-lasting use
  • Includes a stainless steel blade for efficient blending performance
  • Built-in ounce and mL markings for precise measurements
  • Easy cleaning with a simple water and dish soap method
Why Choose This Pitcher?

This pitcher is an essential accessory for any home chef or smoothie enthusiast. Whether you're blending fresh fruits, making creamy shakes, or crushing ice, the 80oz size ensures you have enough capacity for your needs. The combination of PC and stainless steel makes it both sturdy and safe for daily use.

Care Instructions

Although this pitcher is easy to clean, it is not dishwasher safe. After each use, simply fill it with a small amount of water and a drop of dish soap, then blend for 30 seconds to remove any residue. This method keeps your pitcher hygienic and in excellent condition.
