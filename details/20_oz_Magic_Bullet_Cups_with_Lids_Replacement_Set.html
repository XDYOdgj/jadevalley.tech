Felji 2 Pack 20 oz Cups with To Go Lids & Cross Blade & Flat Blade Replacement Set for Magic Bullet Blenders MB1001

Enhance your Magic Bullet Blender experience with this premium replacement set. The new style cups are perfectly compatible with Magic Bullet series cross blade and flat blade attachments, making them ideal for smoothies, shakes, and more.

  • Blade dimensions: 1.5 inches (height) x 3.5 inches (diameter)
  • Package includes: 2 cups, 1 cross blade, 1 flat blade, 4 rubber gaskets
