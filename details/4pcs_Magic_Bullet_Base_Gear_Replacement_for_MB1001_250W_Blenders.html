4PCS Magic Bullet MB1001 Base Gear Replacement

Upgrade your Magic Bullet 250W blender with these high-quality base gear replacements. Perfectly compatible with the Magic Bullet MB1001 model, these parts are designed to fit securely and tightly, ensuring smooth operation and long-lasting performance.

Easy to install and replace, these base gears are ideal for quick maintenance. Simply wrap the blade with a towel, hold it with your left hand, and use the connecting shaft with your right hand to fasten. Rotate the shaft clockwise to remove the broken piece, and tighten counterclockwise during installation to ensure a firm hold.

Whether you're a home cook or a kitchen enthusiast, these replacement parts are a reliable solution for keeping your blender in top condition. The durable construction ensures that each gear is built to last and performs consistently through frequent use.

This package includes four base gears, making it perfect for spare parts or replacing multiple units. Enhance your cooking experience with reliable and efficient blender parts that blend seamlessly with your Magic Bullet.
