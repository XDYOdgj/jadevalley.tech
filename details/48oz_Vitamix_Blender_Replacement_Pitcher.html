LiStarUs 48oz Vitamix Blender Pitcher Replacement

A durable and reliable replacement for Vitamix Advance and BarBoss models. Perfect for home use or professional kitchens.

Product Overview

This 48oz blender pitcher is specifically designed to fit most Vitamix models, including The Quiet One, Advance VM0145, BarBoss, Touch & Go, Blending Station, and more. It offers an efficient and safe way to blend large quantities of ingredients, making it ideal for both personal and commercial use.

Compatibility

Ensure a seamless fit with this 48oz Vitamix container. It is compatible with Vitamix 15978, 16016, 16015, and other Advance series blenders. It is not recommended for use with standard Vitamix models with square bases due to potential minor shaking during operation.

Upgraded Features

This pitcher is engineered with an openable lid, making it easy to add ingredients during blending. Its upgraded stainless steel base provides greater stability and durability compared to plastic. Equipped with hardened stainless steel blades and a double bearing system, it ensures longer blade life and smoother performance. Supports high speeds up to 38,000 RPM for faster grinding and blending. The design also includes an easy pouring spout and a secure lid to maintain contents and prevent spills.

Perfect for Every Kitchen

Whether you're whipping up smoothies, soups, nut butters, or frozen desserts, this 48oz container is up to the task. It features clear measurement markings in ounces, cups, liters, and milliliters, making it simple to measure and adjust ingredients mid-blend. Ideal for home chefs and small business owners looking for consistent, high-quality results.

Easy Maintenance

Keep your blender pitcher in top condition with simple cleaning routines. Simply blend warm water with a few drops of detergent for about 30 seconds, then wipe it dry to avoid water stains. Regular maintenance ensures optimal performance and longevity of your appliance.
