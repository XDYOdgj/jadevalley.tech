Blendin Complete 64oz Replacement Jar Container Set

Enhance your blending experience with the Blendin Complete 64oz Replacement Jar Set, designed for compatibility with Vitamix blenders. This versatile set includes a durable 64oz polycarbonate jar that is virtually unbreakable, making it perfect for everyday use. The jar features a drip-free spout, which helps prevent messy spills and makes cleanup a breeze.

With raised calibrations along the sides, you can easily measure ingredients in ounces, cups, or metric units without needing additional tools. The spill-proof vented lid comes with an attached measuring cap, providing convenience and accuracy during your blending routine. The heavy-duty blade ensures efficient performance, while the retainer nut keeps everything securely in place.

Ideal for smoothies, soups, sauces, and more, this set is built to last and deliver consistent results. However, please note that it is not compatible with Vitamix blenders that use stainless steel jars. Choose Blendin for a reliable, high-quality replacement solution that fits seamlessly into your kitchen workflow.
