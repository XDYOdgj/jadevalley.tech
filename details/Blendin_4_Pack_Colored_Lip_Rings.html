Blendin 4 Pack Colored Lip Rings

Perfectly crafted for Magic Bullet owners, these vibrant lip rings are designed to make your blending experience more organized and enjoyable.

Designed for Magic Bullet Blenders

These colored lip rings are specifically made to fit Magic Bullet models including MB-1001, MB-1001B, MBR-1101, MBR-1701, MBR-1702, and MBR-0301. They are not compatible with Nutribullet, Magic Bullet Mini, or Baby Bullet, ensuring a perfect match for your 250W blender.

Vibrant Colors for Easy Identification

With four different color options, these lip rings help you easily identify whose cup is whose, making your kitchen more organized and fun. Whether you're blending smoothies, shakes, or fresh juices, this added detail brings convenience and style to your daily routine.

Comfort and Durability

These rings are designed with comfort in mind. Their smooth, colored finish makes them easy to use and visually appealing. They also fit standard 3-inch cups, making them versatile for a wide range of Magic Bullet accessories.

Upgrade your Magic Bullet blender with stylish, functional lip rings that make blending more personal and efficient.
